An elderly gentleman is coming into the treatment room. He goes to put his hand on the bench to steady himself but misses and falls, hitting his head on the bench. What do you do?
✔️ **Response to Fall Incident**:

• Stay calm and ensure the patient is safe—do not move them unless in danger.
• Call for help immediately and notify a senior staff member.
• Provide first aid within your scope (apply pressure if bleeding, monitor consciousness).
• Call an ambulance (000) if head trauma is suspected.
• Record the incident in the injury register.
• Notify the supervisor and complete a formal incident report.
• The PCBU must notify WorkCover if it’s a serious injury.
• Follow up with hazard identification to prevent future incidents.
