An elderly gentleman is coming into the treatment room. He goes to put his hand on the bench to steady himself but misses and falls, hitting his head on the bench. What do you do?
Hazard: Fall Incident
Control: Ensure the patient is safe and do not move him unless in danger.
Call for assistance and notify a senior staff member.
Provide first aid within your scope (apply pressure, monitor consciousness).
Call an ambulance (000) if head injury is suspected.
Record the incident in the injury register.
Complete a formal incident report and notify the PCBU.
WorkCover must be notified if the injury is serious.
Conduct a hazard review to prevent recurrence.
